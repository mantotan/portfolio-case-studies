# KunciNFT Marketplace - NFT Marketplace

## Summary

KunciNFT Marketplace was an NFT marketplace built on Binance Smart Chain for primary and secondary NFT sales. The marketplace supported high-profile drops and generated over USD 1M in transactions during the first week.

## Role

Chief Technology Officer at KunciCoin. Led technology strategy and product delivery across NFT marketplace, smart contracts, wallet/blockchain integrations, and related ecosystem initiatives.

## Stack / Domains

- Solidity smart contracts
- Binance Smart Chain
- NFT marketplace architecture
- Wallet integration
- Backend/API systems
- Marketplace transaction flows

## Key Contributions

- Designed and launched KunciNFT Marketplace on Binance Smart Chain.
- Developed and/or directed smart contract work for NFT drops, marketplace transactions, and revenue mechanisms.
- Supported notable drops including Hammersonic 2023 festival NFT in Jakarta.
- Coordinated technical delivery across product, blockchain, and marketplace requirements.

## Results

- Generated over USD 1M in transactions in the first week across primary and secondary sales.
- Helped establish KunciCoin's NFT ecosystem and marketplace infrastructure.

## Challenges

- Building secure and reliable smart contract flows for marketplace activity.
- Supporting NFT drops with high transaction volume and user attention.
- Coordinating product, blockchain, and business requirements under launch pressure.

## What This Demonstrates

- Web3 product leadership
- Solidity and NFT marketplace experience
- High-impact product launch experience
- Blockchain architecture and wallet integration knowledge
