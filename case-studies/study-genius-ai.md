# Study Genius AI - AI Learning Product

## Summary

Study Genius AI is an AI-powered study application that transforms PDFs, images, and documents into personalized flashcards and quizzes. The product is designed to help students and self-learners turn study materials into interactive learning content with spaced repetition and multilingual support.

Website: https://study-genius-ai.hatolabs.com

## Role

Built end-to-end, including:
- Product planning and feature design
- Backend architecture
- Mobile app development
- Landing page development
- AI flashcard and quiz generation workflows
- Spaced repetition logic
- Hosting and deployment
- App distribution preparation

## Stack

- Mobile: React Native
- Landing page: Next.js
- Backend: Node.js, TypeScript, NestJS
- AI: Document-to-flashcard and document-to-quiz generation
- Hosting: Self-managed deployment/hosting setup

## Key Features

- Upload PDFs, images, and documents
- Generate AI-powered flashcards from study materials
- Generate multiple-choice quizzes from uploaded content
- Spaced repetition study workflow
- Progress tracking and review flow
- Offline-capable study experience after sync
- Multilingual support across 10+ languages
- Free-to-start credit-based AI usage model

## Architecture Notes

The product centers on a document-processing pipeline where uploaded study materials are converted into structured learning objects. The backend coordinates content extraction, AI generation, flashcard/quiz storage, review scheduling, and mobile API flows. The system is designed around practical learning workflows instead of generic AI chat.
