# Vexanium Staking & Blockchain Infrastructure

## Summary

Vexanium was a blockchain ecosystem where Hermanto served as CTO, built and led the technology team, supported ICO execution, launched staking/rewards infrastructure, and helped migrate the ecosystem to a custom EOS fork.

## Role

Chief Technology Officer. Built and led the technology team from the ground up, overseeing product architecture, engineering delivery, blockchain infrastructure, and technical strategy.

## Key Contributions

- Directed technology execution for ICO, helping raise over USD 3M from global participants.
- Designed and launched VEX Gift, Indonesia's first staking and rewards platform.
- Helped initiate and lead migration to a custom EOS fork.
- Oversaw ecosystem infrastructure including explorer, wallets, RAM trading, and smart contract support.
- Represented Vexanium across 8+ Southeast Asian cities to support partnerships and ecosystem growth.

## Results

- USD 3M+ raised through ICO support.
- USD 2.6M+ staked through VEX Gift.
- 10K+ monthly active users on the staking/rewards platform.
- Helped create Indonesia's first smart contract-compatible blockchain ecosystem.
